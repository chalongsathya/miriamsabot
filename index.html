My Name is Miriam Sabot, Originally from Gorizia Italy. My background is in sport science and physical education. I have spent the last 4 years living in Chang Mai, Thailand as a professional K1 and Muay Thai fighter. During my time in Thailand I have competed at the highest level in martial arts, fighting locally in Thailand and internationally in Italy, Australia, China and Malaysia.

From this experience, I have the skills and knowledge to train or coach Muay Thai or K1 or western boxing. Furthermore, I have in-depth knowledge to condition or prepare athletes for fights or to improve their physical fitness and overall skills.
